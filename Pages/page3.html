Pacing

By far the biggest problem with the remake is the narrative pacing.
Every section in the original was only as long as it needed to be and then it moved on to the next section,
and once the narrative journey was over it ended right when it was supposed to.
It felt like the perfect length, not feeling too short, but also not overstaying its welcome, it was just right.
It's not an easy balance to find, which is one of many things that makes the original so remarkable.

In contrast the remake's journey is about twice as long as the original's despite telling essentially the same story.
This naturally results in lots of pacing issues, as almost every section of the game feels padded out and overstays its welcome,
which makes getting through the game feel like a slog. Even sections that are a nice addition or even an improvement over the original
are ruined because they last too long and become a chore to get through.

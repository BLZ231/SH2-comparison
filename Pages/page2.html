{Heading: Atmosphere} {Paragraph 1} {The original, like the first Silent Hill, had a masterful atmosphere, in large part because it knew how to be subtle, and had the confidence to let its horror set pieces speak for themselves, and allowed the player’s imagination and fear to do a lot of the work. The remake unfortunately doesn’t quite understand subtlety, and instead of being confident in itself it feels the need to be overtly and blatant scary, which ends up having the opposite effect. Less is more, and because fear is inherently subjective it’s better to focus on cultivating an effective atmosphere than actively trying to be scary, because trying to be scary and failing can end up making you look very silly and/or annoying.} {Atmosphere Comparison Pictures} {Left and Right buttons to scroll through different pairs}
